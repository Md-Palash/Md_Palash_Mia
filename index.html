About

Professional Background

I am a recent Textile Engineering graduate from Bangladesh University of Textiles (BUTEX) with a specialization in Yarn Engineering and a CGPA of 3.42. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in textile manufacturing, yarn production processes, and quality assurance.

Professionally, I have gained practical experience as an Assistant Merchandiser in Central Costing at Snowtex Outerwear Limited, where I handle costing inquiries for international buyers including Decathlon, Haddad, Jack & Jones, and Malacca. I consistently prepare 120-150 cost sheets per month, driving significant efficiency improvements in the costing process.

I am passionate about building a career in textile merchandising, where I can leverage my technical knowledge and practical experience to support efficient production planning, cost control, and global buyer relations in a growth-oriented apparel organization.
