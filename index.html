Academic & Independent Projects

Thesis: Ring vs Vortex Yarn

Compared properties of ring and vortex yarn made from viscose, recycled cotton and polyester. Blended recycled cotton, modal and recycled polyester (30:40:30) for 20 Ne and 30 Ne yarns.

Methodology

Evaluated tensile strength, elongation, CV%, imperfections, hairiness using HVI & AFIS.

Key Finding

Vortex yarns showed better evenness and fewer imperfections; ring yarns had higher strength.
